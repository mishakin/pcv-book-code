% calculates P from K, R and Z
 see PCV (12.34), and additional sign of K33

 Usage:
   P = calc_P_from_KRZ (K,R,Z,[K33])
  
   K   - calibration matrix 
   R   - rotation matrix
   Z   - projection centre
   K33 - sign of K33, optional, default K33 = 1;

   P   - 4 x 3-projection matrix P = K*R*[I|-Z]
